Hunting for the perfect codon aware aligner

May 16, 2014 by Owen
Yesterday I went looking for a well-behaved codon-aware aligner. A question that has been brought up on both Stack Overflow and BioStars. What I discovered is that many software projects that claim to be codon aligners are actually just (1) translating the nucleotide sequence to an amino acid seqeunce, (2) aligning the amino acid sequencing, and (3) mapping the a.a. alignment structure to the nucleotide sequences, in a codon-by-codon fashion.
